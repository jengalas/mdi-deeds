1:434–435
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: (widow) Mary Hamor
   Description: 95 acres (=100 acres, 5 of which are allowed in John Hamor's back lot [see 1:437–438]); shown on Peters Plan as 95 acres, 60 [square] rods
      Beginning at an oak stump, the bound between Jonah Blake and the widow Hamor;
      run first N 56.25° W 390 rods to a fir tree that stands in Mr. Salisbury's line;
      then following said line till the lot is 40 rods wide upon a right angle;
      then running S 56.25 E to the bay;
      then following the shore to the first mentioned bound.
