This parcel includes what is known as Schooner Head.


510:486–487
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: William Lynam
   Description: 100 acres (not specified in deed but shown on Peters Plan)
      Beginning at a birch cast [sic]; in a cove to the south[w]ard of his [William Lynam's] house;
      from said birch we run first S 73° W 120 rods to a stake and stones;
      then N 14° E 160 rods;
      then due east to the shore;
      then following the shore to the first mentioned bounds.
   Note: Although this deed was signed by the DeGregoires on 28 March 1792, it was not recorded in the Hancock County Registry of Deeds until 8 December 1914.
   Grantors obtained this parcel via: grant from the General Court of Massachusetts
