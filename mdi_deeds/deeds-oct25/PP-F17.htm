   Date: 12 December 1837

   Description: 87 acres; lot #56 on Peters Plan

      then N 45° W to the corner bound of said Wasgatt's lot;
      then N 45° E by said Wasgatt's lot to the bound first mentioned.
   Note: These last three directions indicate that the northwest side of this lot and the northwest side of Thomas Wasgatt's lot were slightly offset from each other.
