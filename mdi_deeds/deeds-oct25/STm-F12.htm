54:564
   Date: 20 September 1830
   Town: Mount Desert

   Grantee: William Atherton
   Description: 96 acres, between Long Pond and Echo Lake
      “a certain piece of land lying in said Mount Desert containing ninety-six acres, more or less, and is lot No. 53 on a plan taken by Salem Towne in 1808 and recorded in said County of Hancock”
   Note: William Atherton mortgaged the northern half of this parcel with John Somes Jr. (55:308; 2 April 1831)
