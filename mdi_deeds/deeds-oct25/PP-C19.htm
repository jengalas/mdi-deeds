217:187–189
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: Seth Doane
   Description: two lots conveyed by this deed, both described here. Subsequent deeds on this page follow only the “back lot”.
      first lot:
         Beginning at a spruce tree, the bound between Seth Doane and Timothy Mason;
         run first S 22.5° E 157 rods to a birch tree;
         then N 57.5° E 50 rods to a fir tree;
         then N 22.5° W to the shore;
         then following the shore to the first bound.
      back lot:
         Beginning at a poplar tree on the western sde of Stehen Salisbury’s lot 22 rods from the southwest corner;
         run S 10° W 85 rods to a pine tree;
         then N 80° W 48 rods to a birch tree;
         then N 10° E 125 rods to a stake;
         then S 80° E 50 rods to Stephen Salisbury’ line;
         then following side line to the first bound;
   Grantors obtained this parcel via: grant from the General Court of Massachusetts
