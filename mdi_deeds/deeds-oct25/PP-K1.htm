1:412–413
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Town: Mount Desert
   Grantee: Samuel Hadlock
   Description: 100 acres
      Begin at a birch tree between him [Samuel Hadlock] and John Manchester;
      run first N 78° E 162 rods to a small spruce;
      then N 10° E 124 rods to a small spruce;
      then S 83° W 124 rods to the bay;
      then following the shore to the first mentioned bound.
   Note: There is a 0.5° discrepancy between the compass direction in this deed (N 78° E) and the compass direction of the common boundary line in the deed of John Manchester (1:448–449; N 78.5° E).
   Grantors obtained this parcel via: grant from the General Court of Massachusetts
   Grantee and wife Sally disposed of this parcel via 15:178–179

15:178–179
   Date: 14 April 1803
   Grantors: Samuel and Sally Hadlock
   Town: Mount Desert
   Grantees: Nathan Clark and Lewis Clark
   Description: 100 acres
      Beginning at a stake and stones between him [Samuel Hadlock] and the widow Comfort Manchester;
      run first N 78.5° E 162 rods to a small spruce;
      then N 10° E 124 rods to a small spruce tree;
      then S 83° W 124 rods to the bay;
      then following the shore to the first mentioned bound.
   Note: The 0.5° discrepancy between the compass direction in the above deed (1:412–413) and the compass direction in the deed of John Manchester (1:448–449) has been corrected in the current deed.
