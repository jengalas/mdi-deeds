574:25–27
   Date: 20 November 1922
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Effingham B. Morris and Charles Edward Ingersoll (representing the estate of William Bingham)
   Grantee: George B. Dorr
   Description: 111 acres and 88 square perches; lot #49 on Peters Plan
      Beginning at the extreme north corner of lot #50, of the allotment of the "Bingham Lands" in the town of Bar Harbor (formerly Eden), Hancock County and State of Maine, conveyed to Stephen Higgins Jr.;
      then along the northeast line of said lot and the northeast line of lot #56, [the latter] conveyed to Oliver Higgins, S 45° E to the northwest line of land originally in the name of Thomas Wasgatt;
      then along the northwest line of said Thomas Wasgatt lot, N 45° E, to the south corner of land originally in name of William Wasgatt;
      then by the southwest line of said William Wasgatt lot, northwesterly to the west corner thereof;
      then by the northwest line of said William Wasgatt lot, northeasterly to the south corner of land originally in the name of Benjamin Stanwood;
      then by the southwest line of said Benjamin Stanwood lot, northwesterly to the west corner thereof;
      then by the northwest line of said Benjamin Stanwood lot, northeasterly to the south corner of land originally in the name of Robert Young;
      then along the southwest line of said Robert Young lot, northwesterly to the west corner thereof, in the southeast line of lot #48, conveyed to Richard Higgins;
      then along said line of lot #48, conveyed to Richard Higgins, S 45° W to the place of beginning.
