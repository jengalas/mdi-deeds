95:398–399
   Date: 31 January 1848
   Grantors: Alexander Baring, Henry Baring, and Joseph R. Ingersoll (representing the estate of William Bingham)
   Town: Mount Desert
   Grantee: James Clement
   Description: 102 acres; lot #74 on Peters Plan
      Beginning on the shore at a fir tree, being the southeast corner bound of lot #73 [see note below], deeded to Thomas Savage;
      running by said lot north 314 rods to a spruce tree;
      then east 50 rods to the northwest corner bound of lot #75;
      then south by said lot #75 to the shore;
      then westerly by the shore to the bound first mentioned.
