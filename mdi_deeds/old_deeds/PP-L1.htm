Although Greening Island is not part of Mount Desert Island proper, it is included below because it appeared on the Peters Plan.


1:423
   Date: 13 December 1788
   Town: Southwest Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: Philip Langley
   Description: Somes' Island [now Greening Island]; 77 acres, 46 [square?] rods)
      Lying and being in the fore side of Mount Desert in the mouth of South West Harbor, and likewise in the mouth of Mount Desert River [now Somes Sound].
