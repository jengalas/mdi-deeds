92:314–315
   Date: 24 December 1847
   Grantors: Alexander Baring, Henry Baring, and Joseph R. Ingersoll (representing the estate of William Bingham)
   Town: Mount Desert
   Grantee: Thomas Savage
   Description: 95 acres; lot #73 on Peters Plan
      Beginning at a fir tree on the shore, being the southwest corner bound of lot #74;
      running by said lot north 314 rods to a spruce tree;
      then west 50 rods to the northeast corner bound of lot #70;
      then south by said lot #70 to the shore;
      then easterly by the shore to the bound first mentioned.
   Note: "This lot was sold to George Roberts, and is understood that all taxes upon said lot have been paid".
