61:117–118
   Date: 25 December 1832
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Alexander Baring, Henry Baring, John Richards, Joseph R. Ingersoll, and William Miller (representing the estate of William Bingham)
   Grantee: Joel Emery
   Description: 284.5 acres ; lot #D on Peters Plan
      Beginning at a fir tree, the southeast corner bound of lot #20;
      running S 37° E 200 rods to a stake and stones, 3 rods southerly of the old Eden road;
      then N 53° E 80 rods [see note 1 below] to a hackmatack tree;
      then S 64° E [see note 2 below] 47 rods to a stake and stones on the western sideline of lot #41, occupied by Smith Hopkins;
      then by said lot #41 N 26° E 136 rods to the northwest corner bound of said lot #41;
      then by Thompson lot [see note 3 below] N 78° W 86.5 rods to a birch tree, the southwest corner bound of said Thompson lot;
      then N 11° E 173 rods;
      then S 53° W 295 rods to the bound first mentioned.
   Notes:
      1. Given the first two distances of 200 rods and 80 rods, the map is not to scale.
      2. This direction should be N 64° W.
      3. This direction ignores the apparent difference between the northwest corner of lot #41 and the southeast corner of the Thompson lot.
