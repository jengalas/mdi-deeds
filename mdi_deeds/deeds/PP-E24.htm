1:388–389
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: Benjamin Stanwood
   Description: 100 acres
      Beginning at a stake and stones, the bounds between Robert Young and Benjamin Stanwood;
      from thence running first S 45° W 340 rods to a tree;
      then S 45° E 58 rods to a spruce tree;
      then N 45° E to the shore;
      then following the shore to the first mentioned bounds.
   Grantors obtained this parcel via: grant from the General Court of Massachusetts
   Grantee disposed of this parcel via 38:512

- - - - -

Benjamin Stanwood was the owner of record on the Peters Plan.
