132:305
   Date: 26 December 1868
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: William B. Clymer and Charles Willing (representing the estate of William Bingham)
   Grantee: Lewis H. Bracy
   Description: 65 acres and 36 square rods; lot #81 [see note below] on Peters Plan
      [Bounded] on the north by lot #82, deeded to E. Brewer;
      on the east by lot #86;
      on the south by the seashore;
      on the west by the shore of Otter Creek [now Otter Cove].
   Note: There are two lots numbered 81 on the Peters Plan, this one on the east side of Otter Cove and one bordering the east side of what is now called Stanley Brook in Seal Harbor.
