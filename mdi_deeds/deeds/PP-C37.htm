1:442–443
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: Elkanah Remick
   Description: 100 acres; shown on Peters Plan as 99 acres, 30 [square] rods
      Begin at a birch tree between Levi Higgins and said [Elkanah] Remick;
      run first N 56.25° W 348 rods to a birch tree that stands in the Seth Doane's line;
      then follow said line on 42 rods wide on right angle, to a birch tree;
      then turning and running from said tree S 56.25° E to the shore;
      then following the shore to first mentioned bound.
