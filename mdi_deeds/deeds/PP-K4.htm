   Date: 29 March 1792
   Town: Mount Desert
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: James Richardson Jr.
   Description: 100 acres
      Beginning at a stump, the bounds between him [James Richardson Jr.] and Stephen Sargent;
      run first N 10° E 240 rods;
      then N 80° W 74 rods to John Manchester's line;
      thence following said line to the shore;
      then the shore to the first mentioned bounds.
