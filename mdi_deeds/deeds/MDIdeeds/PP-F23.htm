45:51
   Date: 8 June 1822
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantor: John Rodick
   Grantee: James Hamor
   Description: 24.5 acres, "one equal half of a lot of land granted to Daniel Rodick by Bartholomew DeGregoire" [John Rodick, the grantor in this deed, was likely the son of Daniel Rodick.]
      Beginning at a stake and stones at the shore, being the northeast corner bound between Daniel Rodick and Thomas Wasgatt Jr.;
      from thence running due south 203 rods to a spruce tree;
      then due west 18.5 rods;
      then due north to the aforesaid shore;
      then by said shore to the first-mentioned bound.
   Note: This deed also included one-half of Bar Island.
