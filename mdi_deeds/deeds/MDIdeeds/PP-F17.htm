   Date: 12 December 1837
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Alexander Baring, Henry Baring, Joseph R. Ingersoll, and William Miller (representing the estate of William Bingham)
   Grantee: Oliver Higgins
   Description: 87 acres; lot #56 on Peters Plan
      Beginning on the side line of Thomas Wasgatt's settler's lot, near the northwest corner bound of the same;
      running N 45° W about 65 rods to a hemlock tree, the east corner bound of lot #50;
      then S 45° W 200 rods to a spruce tree, the north corner bound of lot #57;
      then S 45° E 70 rods to a spruce tree;
      then N 45° E to the head line of said Thomas Wasgatt's lot [see note below];
      then N 45° W to the corner bound of said Wasgatt's lot;
      then N 45° E by said Wasgatt's lot to the bound first mentioned.
   Note: These last three directions indicate that the northwest side of this lot and the northwest side of Thomas Wasgatt's lot were slightly offset from each other.
