1:371–372
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: Thomas Wasgatt Jr./2nd [Jr. on plan; 2nd in deed]
   Description: 2 lots: first lot (140 acres) and back lot (60 acres); This page follows the back lot.
      first lot (140 acres):
         Beginning at the stake and stones, the bounds between him and Daniel Rodick;
         from thence run first due south 2[…] rods to a spruce tree;
         then due west 37 rods to Mr. Higgins’ line;
         then south on said line 48 rods;
         then N 43° E 50 rods or until […] come to the brook;
         then following said brook to the shore;
         then following the shore to the first mentioned bounds.
      back lot (60 acres):
         [Begin] at the southwest corner of Ezra Leland’s lot;
         run first S 45° W 180 rods to a stake;
         then N 45° W 33.5 rods;
         then N 45° E 178 rods to Leland’s line;
         then following said line to the first bound.
   Grantors obtained this parcel via: grant from the General Court of Massachusetts
   Grantee disposed of the back lot this parcel via 2:463–464

2:463–464
   Date: 23 June 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantor: Thomas Wasgatt [no designation of Jr. or 2nd]
   Grantee: Samuel Hull
   Description: 60 acres
      [Begin] at the southwest corner of Ezra Leland’s lot;
      run first S 45° W 180 rods to a stake;
      then N 45° E 33.5 rods; [see note 1 below]
      then N 45° E 128 rods to Leland’s line; [see note 2 below]
      then following said line to the first bound.
   Notes:
      1. This deed incorrectly reads N 45° E, which would be a retracing of a portion of the previous boundary line. The correct direction is N 45° W, as given for the same line in 1:371–372 above.
      2. This distance should be 178 rods, given that distance in 1:371–372 above and the relative dimensions of the parcel shown on the Peters Plan.
