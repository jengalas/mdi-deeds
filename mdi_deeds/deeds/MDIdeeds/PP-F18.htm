57:367
   Date: 15 March 1833
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: James Hamor and Clarissa Hamor
   Grantee: Asa Higgins
   Description: 100 acres, more or less; “being the same lot of land formerly conveyed to William Wasgatt by DeGregoire by deed bearing date March twenty-eighth, A.D. seventeen hundred & ninety-two”.
      Beginning at a stake and stone, being the bound between Stephen Higgins 2nd and Amos Richardson;
      running S 45° W 374 rods to a cedar tree;
      then S 45° E 44 rods to a spruce tree;
      then N 45° E to the shore;
      then following the shore to the first mentioned bound.
   Note: Asa Higgins used this parcel as collateral for a mortgage (72 :372–373; January 1842) with James Hamor. The discharge was recorded in the margin of 72:373 and was reported to have been on 31 January 1852.
   Grantors obtained this parcel via 54:498–499
   Grantee disposed of this parcel via 105:320, 135:207, and 139:3

105:320
   Date: 15 September 1857
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantor: Asa Higgins
   Grantee: Lydia Thurber
   Description: two parcels totaling 2 acres, more or less.
      First Parcel:
         Beginning at the highway on the line between Amos Richardson and Asa Higgins;
         then northeast 37 rods to a pear tree;
         then northwest 6 rods to a stake;
         then southwest 37 rods to the highway;
         then following the highway to the first bound.
      Second Parcel:
         Beginning at the highway within one rod of Amos Richardson’s line;
         then west 14 rods to a stake;
         then northwest 6 rods to a stake;
         then northeast 14 rods to the highway;
         then following the highway to the first bound.
