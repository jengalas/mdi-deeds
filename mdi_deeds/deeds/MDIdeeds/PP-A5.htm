27:12–13
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: Daniel Richardson
   Description: 36 acres
      Beginning at a fir tree, the bound between him [Daniel Richardson] and John Thomas;
      run first S 25° E 100 rods;
      then S 88° E to Mr. Hadley’s line;
      then N 20° W to the shore;
      then following the shore to the first bound.
   Grantors obtained this parcel via: grant from the General Court of Massachusetts
   Grantee disposed of this parcel via 64:523
