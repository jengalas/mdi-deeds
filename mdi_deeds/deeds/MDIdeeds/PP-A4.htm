6:258–259
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: John Thomas
   Description: 61 acres 13 [square?] rods by deed; 65 acres 130 [square?] rods by Peters Plan
      Beginning at a fir tree near the great rock;
      runs first S 25° E 100 rods;
      then S 88° E to Mr. Hadley’s line;
      then S 20° E 80 rods to the to the head of Mr. Hadley’s line to a spruce tree;
      then S 46° W to a spruce tree at the head of Mr. John Thomas’s lot;
      then N 37° W to the shore;
      then following the shore to the first-mentioned bound.
   Grantors obtained this parcel via: grant from the General Court of Massachusetts
   Grantee disposed of this parcel via 6:262
