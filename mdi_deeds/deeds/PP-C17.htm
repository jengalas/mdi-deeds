   Date: 20 June 1836

   Description: 104 acres; lot #13 on Peters Plan

      then northwesterly by said Salisbury's lot to the west corner bound of the same;
      then northeasterly to the side line of William Mason's lot;
      then north by said Mason's lot to the head line of Joseph Hopkins's lot;
      then westwardly by said Hopkins's to the side line of Eben Leland's lot;
      then southwestwardly by said Leland's lot to the southeast corner bound of the same;
      then westwardly by the same lot to a creek;
      then down said creek to the Northeast Creek, aforementioned;
      then down said Northeast Creek to the bound first mentioned.
