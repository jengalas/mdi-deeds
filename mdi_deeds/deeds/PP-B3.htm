151:91–92
   Date: 15 April 1875
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Charles Willing and Chapman Biddle (representing the estate of William Bingham)
   Grantee: K. K. Thompson
   Description: 89 acres, more or less; lot #10 on Peters Plan
      Beginning at the north corner of lot #9 of the allotment of the lands of the Bingham estate in Eden Township aforesaid conveyed to Nahum Cousins;
      then along the northeast line of said lot S 48° E to a maple;
      then along a marsh by the several courses and distances thereof (in the main northeasterly) to the shore;
      then along said shore by the several courses and distances thereof north and westerly to a corner of a lot conveyed to Joseph Mayo;
      then along lines of said Mayo lot S 31° E to the eastern corner thereof and S 59° W to the place of beginning.
