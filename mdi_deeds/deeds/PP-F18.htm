1:360–361
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: William Wasgatt
   Description: 100 acres
      Beginning at a stake and stones, being the bounds between Benjamin Stanwood and William Wasgatt;
      running first S 45° W 374 rods to a cedar tree;
      then S 35° E 44 rods to a spruce tree;
      then N 45° E to the shore;
      then following the shore to the first mentioned bounds.
   Grantors obtained this parcel via: grant from the General Court of Massachusetts
   Grantee disposed of this parcel via 51:282–283
