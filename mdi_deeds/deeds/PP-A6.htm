This parcel contains Hadley Point, the northernmost point on Mount Desert Island.


1:433–434
   Date: 28 March 1792
   Town: Bar Harbor
   Grantors: Bartholomy DeGregoire and Maria Theresa DeGregoire
   Grantee: Simeon Hadley
   Description: 100 acres
      Beginning at an oak tree, which is a bound between Daniel Richardson and the said Simeon [Hadley];
      and running first S 15° E 200 rods to a spruce tree;
      then N 37° E 166 rods to the shore;
      then following the shore to the first mentioned bounds.
