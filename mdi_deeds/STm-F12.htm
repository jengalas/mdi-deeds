54:564
   Date: 20 September 1830
   Town: Mount Desert
   Grantors: John Q. Adams, Nathaniel Curtis, and Alicia Boylston, executors and trustees of the last will of Ward N[icholas]. Boylston
   Grantee: William Atherton
   Description: 96 acres, between Long Pond and Echo Lake
      "a certain piece of land lying in said Mount Desert containing ninety-six acres, more or less, and is lot No. 53 on a plan taken by Salem Towne in 1808 and recorded in said County of Hancock"
   Grantee disposed of this parcel via 55:308 (northern half) and likely through his will to son Onslow A. Atherton, who, in turn, disposed of it via 145:416 (southern half)
